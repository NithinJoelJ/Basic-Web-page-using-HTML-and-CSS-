Who is Doraemon?

Doraemon is a popular Japanese manga and anime character created by Fujiko F. Fujio. The character first appeared in a manga series in 1969 and later became an anime series. Doraemon is a robotic cat sent back in time from the 22nd century to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi. The main storyline revolves around Doraemon's attempts to improve Nobita's life and prevent him from making mistakes that could lead to a difficult future. Doraemon is equipped with various futuristic gadgets from his 4D pocket, which he uses to assist Nobita and his friends in solving problems and navigating daily life. The character has become a cultural icon in Japan and has gained popularity worldwide. The Doraemon series has been adapted into various films, television shows, and merchandise over the years.
